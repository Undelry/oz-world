## Risks & Challenges

### Technical
- Solana scalability (may need L2 or migration)
- Smart contract vulnerabilities
- Oracle manipulation attacks

### Financial
- Early stage volatility
- Liquidity attacks
- Market manipulation

### Regulatory
- Global regulatory uncertainty
- Potential bans in some jurisdictions
- Legal status of founder as minor

### Social
- Bootstrapping network effects
- Trust building in early stages
- Competition from Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.

### Founder Risks
- Single point of failure (Phase 0)
- Youth/inexperience (though also an asset)
- Health/accident/burnout

---

## Mitigation

1. **Transparency**: Publish everything. Hide nothing.
2. **Community**: Transition to distributed governance ASAP.
3. **Legal**: Consult lawyers as scale grows.
4. **Technical**: Open source all code, welcome audits.
5. **Personal**: Maintain health, build a team, avoid single-point dependency.
